# VepMod

A new enemy that makes you hallucinate your teammates.

## The Whispral

When it catches you, your teammates vanish. Instead, you see strange figures that look just like them - same colors,
same names.
They wander around. Sometimes they stare at you. And they speak with your friends' actual voices.

## Features

- Hallucinations mimic your teammates
- Uses real voice recordings from your lobby
- They stalk and watch you
- Full multiplayer support

## Requirements

- [REPOLib](https://thunderstore.io/c/repo/p/Zehs/REPOLib/)
